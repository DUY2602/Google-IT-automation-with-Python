1. Identify the Problem:
    + Gather infomation from logs, error messages, users,...
    + Have a look at the problem

2. Reproduce the issue:
    + Recreate the bug in maintaining environment

3. Narrow down the cause:
    + Use elimination (divide and conquer)
    + Test hypotheses step by step

4. Test the hypotheses:
    + Try small changes, validate outcomes

5. Implement solution:
    + Apply a fix or maintenance

6. Verify fix:
    + Ensure the issue is fully resolved
    + Retest the entire system

7. Document the process:
    + Record the cause, the solution, and steps taken for future use
